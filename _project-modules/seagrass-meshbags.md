## Overview
Seagrass mesh bags are a rapid way to destructively sample organisms associated with the seagrass canopy (e.g., small invertebrates, macroalgae).

### Measured Parameters
  - Epifaunal composition
  - Epifauna dry weight
  - Macroalgal dry weight
