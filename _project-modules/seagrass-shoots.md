## Overview
Individual shoots of seagrass provide data on canopy characteristics and estimates of primary productivity.

### Measured Parameters
  - Blade length (mm)
  - Blade width (mm)
  - Sheath length (mm)
  - Disease lesions (number and length in mm)
  - Grazing scars (number)
  - Fouling biomass (mg)
